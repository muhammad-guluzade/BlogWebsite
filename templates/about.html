This is a simple blog website.

You can register or log in if you have registered to the website already. A user who is logged in can make posts and leave comments, as well as delete and edit the posts that they made.

In order to delete a post made by you, you just need to press a small cross next to your post on the main page. In order to edit a post you need to press "edit" button on the page where you view your post's details.
